Health Assessment Service (HAS)

{{ serviceName }}

Conduct an assessment

A Health Care Professional will check the rota to see what assessments they have to complete that day.

Health Care Professional assessments can take place over the phone or face-to-face. They will navigate the journey and add notes based on the conversation.

Depending on the level of the Health Care Professional (HCP), they pass it to a Senior HCP to audit. This process is done offline using spreadsheets.

Once the audit is complete, the HCP who conducted the assessment can submit the recommendation report. The status will automatically update to Report Ready.

The Health Care Professional (HCP) has completed their actions, an assessment outcome and the Health Care Professionals report will be automatically sent as data to the new PIP Service for a Case Manager to pick up and make their decision.

If the Health Care Professional report can't be sent as data, a PDF will be automatically uploaded for a Case Manager to view.

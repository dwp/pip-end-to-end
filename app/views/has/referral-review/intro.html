Review referrals

Once NCAT have built the referral, HCPs will review the referral including any supporting evidence that's in HAS or in the claimant Sharepoint folder. 

HCPs will then record the outcome of that review and make a recommendation of how to progress the referral: 

  • gather more evidence
  • require a paper based review
  • require a telephone assessment
  • require face to face assessment
  • SREL case

Healthcare professionals (HCP) update the timeline to say a HCP review is complete.

The case has now been passed to the NCAT team who need to book an assessment.

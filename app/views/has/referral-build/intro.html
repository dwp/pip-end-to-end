Build new referrals

This is the start of the health assessment service journey where the Non Clinical Admin Team (NCAT) build new referrals.

New PIP referrals appear in the 'New referrals' area in HAS. NCAT perform a series of online and offline tasks to build the referrals.

Team Chaffinch are responsible for certain workflow activities that enables users to navigate to, action and complete these tasks.

Some of the workflow activities include:

  • Assigning a new referral to a caseload
  • Moving a referral to another caseload
  • Changing the status of a referral
  • Navigating between caseloads and statuses
  • Finding a claimant

Caseloads are managed at a local level based on where the claim is in the journey, the team, role or individual responsible.

Once NCAT have built the referral, Health care professionals (HCPs) will review the referral including any supporting evidence that's in the Health Assessment Service (HAS) and the Claimant Management Sheet kept in the claimant Sharepoint folder.

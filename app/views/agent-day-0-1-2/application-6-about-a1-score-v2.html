Preparing food

Health information gather (PIP2)

Asked in application

Does your condition affect you preparing food, or prevent you from doing so?

Response in application

I find it increasingly difficult to cook a hot meal due to heavy pans and opening cans.

Standing for long periods hurts my legs, so I prepare veg sitting down.

I rely heavily on my partner to do the cooking.

Assessment report findings

Difficulty reported is consistent with his diagnosis of fibromyalgia.

He is prescribed medication for nerve pain and is known to the pain management team, though is currently just having phone calls with a clinical psychologist to manage his pain.

He reports he is able to do things like make a sandwich, some days use a microwave and lift a kettle.

It is reasonable to suggest he requires aids to complete this reliably.

The need for assistance has been considered and ruled out as he has no medication prescribed for severe pain, though has input from rheumatology.

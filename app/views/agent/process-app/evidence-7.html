Conditions

 

Condition or disability: Fibromyalgia

Approximate start date: 14 January 2018 (over 6 years ago)

Condition or disability: Anxiety

Approximate start date: 14 Months ago

Daily living functional restriction

Preparing food

 

Asked in application

Does your condition affect you preparing food, or prevent you from doing so?

Response in application

I find it increasingly difficult to cook a hot meal due to heavy pans and opening cans.

Standing for long periods hurts my legs, so I prepare veg sitting down.

I rely heavily on my partner to do the cooking.

Taking nutrition

 

Asked in application

Does your condition affect you eating and drinking?

Response in application

On a bad day, I struggle to cut things because it hurts to lift my arms to cut food.

Opening cheese packets or crisps is always a problem.

I drink from a little lightweight mug.

Managing therapy or
monitoring a health condition

 

Asked in application

Does your condition affect you managing your treatments?

Response in application

I have a pill box with my tablets in and an alarm on my phone to remind me, otherwise I would forget. My husband checks I have taken them.

I go to a chiropractor when I need to. Also, I have a leg message on a weekly basis.

Washing and bathing

 

Asked in application

Does your condition affect you washing and bathing?

Response in application

I am able to bathe myself but my husband washes my legs and feet for me on a bad day. He also dries my lower half.

My husband washes my hair. I struggle to do this myself and it takes over an hour. I don't use a hair dryer as it hurts too much to hold my arms up.

Managing toilet needs or incontinence

 

Asked in application

Does your condition affect you using the toilet or managing incontinence?

Response in application

I have fallen a few times due to my legs being unsteady.

My bathroom is downstairs so it's a bit of a way to go. On these occasions, my husband makes sure I'm ok.

Dressing and undressing

 

Asked in application

Tell us about difficulties you have dressing and undressing, and how you manage them

Response in application

On a bad day, I struggle with shoes and other clothes. My clothes feel tight, particularly trousers. I wear PJs most of the time.

Communicating verbally

 

Asked in application

Does your condition affect you talking, listening and understanding?

Response in application

I can't cope with too many conversations. I can't follow the conversation.

I tend not to go to many places with too many people and decline offers of family or social events.

Reading and understanding
signs, symbols and words

 

Asked in application

Does your condition affect your ability to read?

Response in application

Person indicated that their condition does not affect them reading.

Engaging with other
people face to face

 

Asked in application

Does your condition affect you mixing with other people?

Response in application

As before, I avoid these situations. Too many people make my brain go fuzzy.

It completely overwhelms me if I do go. I have to rest for a good couple of days afterwards.

Making budgeting decisions

 

Asked in application

Tell us about the difficulties you have with managing your money

Response in application

Person indicated that their condition does not affect them managing their money.

Mobility functional restriction

Planning and
following journeys

 

Asked in application

Does your condition affect you planning and following journeys?

Response in application

It takes a lot of planning to go out. Where will I park? How long will I stay? Will I be able to walk when I get there? Can I carry anything?

If I have to go to two places a few miles apart it causes me stress. Stress to the point where I just come home.

I don't go out unless I really have to.

Moving around

 

Asked in application

Does your condition affect you moving around?

Response in application

If I'm having a bad day, I don't go out. In my house, I hold on to things to steady me from falling.

If I'm able to walk my small dog, I do small walks and stop a few times.

I find road surface much easier than grass or shiny surfaces.

Based on your answers, you may not be eligible for PIP

Most people over State Pension age cannot get PIP.

You might be eligible for Attendance Allowance instead.

You might still be eligible for PIP if:

  • you had a PIP award that stopped in the last year
  • you're getting Disability Living Allowance or it stopped in the last year

You can still claim PIP if you think you're eligible but you cannot claim online.

Confirm your identity

You will need information from 2 sources to confirm your identity online.

You can choose from:

  • a valid UK passport
  • a UK driving licence
  • a payslip from the last 3 months
  • a current P60
  • your Self Assessment tax return - the most recent payment you made
  • a current tax credits claim - a recent payment you received
  • your credit record - for example, the date you opened a bank account

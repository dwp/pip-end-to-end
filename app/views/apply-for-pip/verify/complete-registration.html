Complete your-registration

Register online so you can securely sign in whenever you use the Apply for PIP service. Only continue if you have:

  • already started your claim for Personal Independence Payment (PIP) by phone and have received an email from the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP)
  • partly completed your registration online

Before you start

You will need:

  • your National Insurance number
  • your email address
  • your mobile phone

What happens next

You will need to enter a few personal details. We will then send a 6 digit code to your mobile phone and another to your email address.

You will also need to create a password.

Whenever you sign in we will send a new security code to your mobile phone or email address.

We will check your details against our records to make sure we are dealing with the right person. This also helps protect you from fraud.

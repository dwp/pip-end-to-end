Case worker - manual checks

Once the claimant has registered, the service will set a date of claim and timer for completion. A case worker will manually check against thin slice rules based on what the claimant has entered.

If the rules to retain it in the new service are met and the service isn't at capacity it will continue through the thin slice.

A case worker will also complete a series of eligibility checks using the new service. They will check other systems like Customer Information System (CIS) / Searchlight.

If the answers to the eligibility question make it complicated, then it will be disallowed from the thin slice and routed back to PIPCS for Business as Usual.

Once the Health Information Gather has been completed by the claimant and there are no further tasks, the case is referred to the Health Assessment Service (HAS).
